Atomic Cells

#ld39 (Running out of Power)

In Atomic Cells you must outwit the enemy and overpower them before you run out of energy.

pre-game

Before the game starts, you will place four energy cells anywhere on the map.

basic gameplay

Every energy cell can fire protons at other energy cells, and every proton fired will drain some of its energy.

If the proton is targetting a friendly cell, it will transfer the energy. If its targeting an enemy cell, it will damage the shield surrounding the cell. If the shield goes down, the cell will belong the other team.

winning

In order to win the game, you must remove all enemy cells from the map. You can do this by either gaining control of them all, or having them run out of energy and explode.
